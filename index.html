Do you provide full KDP publishing support? +

Yes! I offer complete KDP publishing support including manuscript formatting, cover design, interior layout, and publishing guidance to ensure your book is ready for Amazon.

Can you design children book covers? +

Absolutely! I specialize in colorful, engaging children's book covers and interior illustrations that captivate young readers and their parents.

Do you provide source files? +

Yes, all packages include source files in PSD, AI, or other editable formats so you have full ownership and flexibility with your designs.

Can you redesign old book covers? +

Of course! I frequently help authors refresh and modernize their existing book covers to boost sales and improve market appeal.
